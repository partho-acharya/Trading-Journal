Trading Rules & Psychology

Following these principles can help you become a consistently profitable trader:

Trading Psychology

  • Control emotions - fear and greed are your biggest enemies
  • Accept losses as part of the business
  • Be patient - wait for your setup
  • Don't revenge trade after a loss
  • Maintain discipline even during winning streaks

Risk Management

  • Risk only 1-2% of your capital per trade
  • Always use stop losses
  • Maintain a positive risk-reward ratio (at least 1:2)
  • Diversify your portfolio
  • Never add to a losing position

Trading Plan Essentials

  • Define your entry and exit criteria
  • Know your profit targets and stop losses before entering
  • Keep a trading journal and review it regularly
  • Stick to your strategy - avoid impulsive decisions
  • Set realistic profit expectations

Common Pitfalls to Avoid

  • Overtrading - quality over quantity
  • Chasing the market - wait for pullbacks
  • Ignoring market context and sentiment
  • Trading without a clear edge
  • Letting winners turn into losers
